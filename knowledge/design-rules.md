# Design Rules — Sharingan Scan Log
> Recon on master sites. Copy the *rules*, not the pixels. Reusable worksheet per site.

---

## Scan #1 — apple.com  (scanned 2026-06-29)
**Target rank:** Monarch (gold standard for restraint)

**1. COLORS**
- Background: `#ffffff` (pure white)
- Text: `#1d1d1f` (near-black)
- ONE accent: `#0071e3` (Apple blue) — only on buttons/links
- Note: all real color comes from the product photo, not the UI.

**2. FONTS**
- One family only: SF Pro (system font). Headline weight 600, body lighter. No second font.

**3. SHAPE**
- Buttons: pill (`border-radius: 980px`), tight padding (`8px 15px`)
- Massive whitespace, centered hero.

**4. SECTIONS (top → bottom)**
1. Nav (logo + product links + search/bag)
2. Hero (product name + one-line tagline + 2 CTAs + photo)
3. Stacked product blocks (alternating white / black backgrounds)

**5. THE ONE RULE (what kills slop)**
Silence everything so ONE thing pops. 2 neutrals + 1 accent + 1 font + air.

**6. STEAL THIS**
The discipline: pick one accent, one font, give it space. Restraint = premium.

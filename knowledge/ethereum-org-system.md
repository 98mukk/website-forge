# Ethereum.org system — code + ART DIRECTION (the pictures are the brand)

ETHEREUM.ORG'S IMAGERY THESIS: the illustrations ARE the brand. Custom dreamlike illustrated worlds (a futuristic city, floating structures, soft rounded characters) carry all the personality while the UI stays simple. Rule: invest in one signature illustration style and reuse it everywhere; the interface is just a quiet frame around the art.

ETHEREUM.ORG'S ILLUSTRATION STYLE RULES: soft pastel palettes (peach #F0CDC2, lavender #C9B3F5, mint #B8FAF6, periwinkle #88AAF1) anchored by ONE deep saturated brand blue (#1616B4). Whimsical-optimistic mood, rounded organic shapes, no photorealism, no stock photography, no faces as subjects. Illustrations depict abstract concepts (community, building, connection) as physical scenes.

ETHEREUM.ORG'S IMAGE PLACEMENT SIGNATURE: card illustrations are pinned absolute to the bottom-right corner, sized about 2/3 of card height, and BLEED OFF the card edges (absolute positioning with negative offsets). The art escapes its container — makes flat cards feel alive and layered. Hero image is a full-width panoramic illustration behind the headline.

ETHEREUM.ORG'S COLOR-WASH TECHNIQUE: section and card backgrounds use gradient tints of many hues at ~10% alpha (gradient-to colors ending in 1a) over white — each card gets its own faint color world without breaking the one-brand-blue rule. Full-strength color lives only in illustrations and the primary CTA.

ETHEREUM.ORG'S TYPE SYSTEM: Inter for UI/body plus IBM Plex Mono for technical/numeric accents. Headlines are HUGE and heavy: text-5xl to text-7xl at font-black (900 weight), tight leading (0.9-1.1), text-balance for clean rag. Statement-sized type ("The internet that belongs to you") over long marketing copy.

ETHEREUM.ORG'S MOTION: same restraint family as Apple — cubic-bezier(.4,0,.6,1) and (.4,0,.2,1), sub-350ms, transitions on hover/reveal only, nothing looping. The liveliness comes from the art, not from animation.

ETHEREUM.ORG'S LAYOUT: white/near-white ground, generous padding, cards with radius-lg to radius-2xl (8-16px), alternating full-width story sections with 2-3 card grids. Every section pairs one big heavy headline + short body + one illustration. Content max-width contained, illustrations allowed to break the grid.

IMAGERY-AS-INTEL LESSON (for the Forge): when a reference site's power is its pictures, capture the ART DIRECTION as rules: subject style, palette, mood, composition, where images sit, how they crop. Those rules then drive image generation (Nano Banana) so generated art matches the world, instead of random stock.

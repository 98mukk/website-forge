# Hedera.com Design System (extracted from live CSS, July 2026)

HEDERA'S COLOR SYSTEM: The entire palette lives in one :root token block with named colors: --pure-white #fff, --pure-black #000, --charcoal #11151d, --dark-grey #9b9b9d, --cobalt #2d84eb, --cobalt-light #5aa6ff, --cobalt-grey #e1e8ef, --azure #0031ff, --ultraviolet #8259ef, --ultraviolet-light #b47aff, --indigo #00156e, --violet #4f46e5, plus two translucent fills, rgba(130,89,239,.2) as a light purple wash and rgba(255,255,255,.1) as a glass panel on dark. The scheme is a black-dominant dark site where every accent sits on a single violet-to-blue axis (ultraviolet, violet, indigo, azure, cobalt), with #5eead4 teal as a rare secondary pop. Lesson for builders: pick one hue axis for all accents, name every token semantically in :root, and let pure black carry the sophistication.

HEDERA'S TYPE SYSTEM: One typeface does everything: Styrene A, a grotesque sans, applied at light weights for scale. The desktop scale is h1 68px/68px weight 300 with letter-spacing -0.01em, h2 44px/50px weight 300 with -0.01em, h3 24px/34px weight 300, h4 18px/28px weight 400, body 16px/26px weight 400; on mobile h1 collapses to 32px/36px with tracking reset to 0. Note that line-height equals font-size at the h1 level (68/68), giving dense, solid headline blocks. The lesson: big headlines get LIGHTER weight (300) and tighter negative tracking, never bolder, and a single-family system reads more premium than font pairing.

HEDERA'S SIGNATURE NUMERALS: The most recognizable move is the .numbers class: Styrene A at font-weight 100, font-size 124px, line-height 124px, letter-spacing -0.05em, responsively scaled with clamp(75px, 8.75vw, 124px). A companion .numbers-gradient class paints them with linear-gradient(90deg, var(--ultraviolet) 0%, var(--cobalt) 100%) using background-clip: text and color: transparent. Stats sections ("Proof in the numbers") become hero moments. Lesson: hairline-weight display numerals with gradient-clipped text turn boring metrics into brand identity; use clamp for fluid scaling instead of breakpoint jumps.

HEDERA'S GRADIENT LANGUAGE: Gradients are radial and deliberately anchored OFF-canvas so only the glow edge is visible, for example radial-gradient(108.41% 108.41% at 108.41% -8.07%, var(--ultraviolet) 11.99%, var(--indigo) 82.47%) places the light source beyond the top-right corner, and radial-gradient(100% 378.03% at 0% 0%, #8259ef 0%, #000 100%) fades ultraviolet into pure black from the top-left. Card and section backgrounds always terminate in --indigo #00156e or #000, never a light color, so glows feel like light hitting a dark surface. Lesson: position radial gradient centers outside the element (over 100% or negative coordinates) and always fade to black to get the premium dark-tech glow instead of a cheap two-color fill.

HEDERA'S BUTTON AND RADIUS SYSTEM: Buttons are full pills: font-size 14px Styrene A weight 400, padding 9px/11px block and 21px inline, border-radius 36px (42px on larger variants), 1px border. Variants are .btn-purple (solid #8259ef with white text) and .btn3-white-outline (transparent with 1px white border). Cards use border-radius 30px and 10px, avatars 50%; small UI bits sit at 3px to 6px. The hover is the killer detail: a ::before pseudo-element sized calc(100% + 2px), holding radial-gradient(157.78% 160.69% at 128.78% -0.69%, var(--cobalt) 1.44%, var(--violet) 46.97%, var(--indigo) 99.05%), starts at opacity 0 and fades in with transition: opacity .5s ease. Lesson: reveal a pre-rendered gradient layer via opacity instead of animating background-color, which is both smoother and GPU-cheap.

HEDERA'S MOTION SYSTEM: Timing is conservative and layered: transition: color .3s ease, border-color .3s ease, background-color .5s ease on buttons, opacity .5s ease for gradient reveals, .2s to .3s for transforms and menu movement, and transform .2s ease with box-shadow .2s ease on lifting cards. The only custom curves are cubic-bezier(.4, 0, .6, 1) for standard easing and cubic-bezier(.28, .84, .42, 1) for a springy overshoot on playful elements. Ambient motion comes from an autoplaying hero background video (hero_tall_2mb.mp4), canvas elements, and Lottie animations rather than CSS keyframe gimmicks. Lesson: keep interaction transitions between 200ms and 500ms with plain ease, stagger property durations (color faster than background), and outsource ambient motion to video or Lottie.

HEDERA'S ART DIRECTION: Imagery splits into three strict lanes: an abstract dark 3D-rendered hero video with violet and cobalt light streaks, a monochrome trust wall of SVG partner marks named as uniform "logo box" tiles (Google, IBM, Dell, FedEx, Deutsche Telekom, LG, accenture, Chainlink Labs, Mondelez, Nomura), and real photography of governing-council humans in 3:2 crops plus 270x300 portrait cutouts for testimonials. There is no illustration and no stock-photo filler; everything is either rendered light, a logo, or a real person. Lesson: enterprise credibility is built by pairing abstract rendered motion (innovation) with dense grids of recognizable grayscale logos and genuine human faces (trust), never generic illustrations.

HEDERA'S INFO ARCHITECTURE: The homepage narrative runs h1 "A future built on enterprise trust", then "Fortune 1000 companies choose Hedera" (logo wall), "HBAR, the native cryptocurrency on Hedera" (product), "Proof in the numbers" (giant numeral stats), "World-leading institutions govern the network" (council photos), "The foundational trust layer" and "Why Hedera is the trust layer of the digital economy" (differentiation), closing with "Join in and start building" and "Ready to get started?" (developer CTAs). Social proof appears BEFORE the product explanation. Lesson: for trust-critical products, sequence the page as claim, borrowed credibility, product, quantified proof, governance/human faces, differentiation, then action, and repeat the CTA at the end.

HEDERA'S RECOGNIZABILITY FORMULA: Strip everything else away and Hedera is identifiable from five moves: pure black canvas with off-canvas ultraviolet #8259ef radial glows fading to #000, a single grotesque typeface (Styrene A) at light 300 weight with negative tracking, 124px weight-100 gradient-clipped numerals, 36px-radius pill buttons whose gradients fade in via opacity, and a grayscale Fortune 1000 logo wall against the dark. Nothing uses more than one hue family per surface, and no surface is mid-gray, it is either near-black (#000, #11151d) or white. Lesson: a memorable system is a small set of extreme, repeated decisions (one font, one hue axis, one radius language, one glow style) applied without exception.

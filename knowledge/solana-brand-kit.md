# Solana Foundation Brand Guidelines Sept 2025 (official PDF scan)

SOLANA BRAND KIT, DOCUMENT SCOPE: The official "Solana Foundation Brand Guidelines" (Sept 2025) is a 2-page trademark-law document, not a visual style guide. It contains no hex colors, font names, clearspace measurements, or sizing ratios; those visual specs live on the Solana Brand and Press page, which the Guidelines reference as the canonical, updateable source for all "Solana Marks" (the SOLANA word mark, the "S" Logomark, and all other Foundation trademarks, names, logos, and slogans). Lesson: for Solana visual tokens (colors, type, spacing), pull from the live Brand and Press assets or the site scan; the official PDF governs legal usage only.

SOLANA BRAND KIT, THE MARKS AND OWNERSHIP: The Solana Marks are the exclusive property of the Solana Foundation, a not-for-profit dedicated to the decentralization, growth, and security of the Solana network. Using the Marks grants zero ownership or independent rights; you may not claim rights via common-law use, and you may not register any Solana Mark, alone or combined with your own or third-party trademarks, for your own or anyone else's benefit. The Foundation can, at its sole discretion, order you to stop all use if it deems your use contrary to its mission or the Guidelines. Lesson: treat client use of the Solana logo as a revocable license, never an asset they own.

SOLANA BRAND KIT, LOGO INTEGRITY LAW: The Marks may not be used in any manner that modifies, alters, changes the scale of, skews, rotates, or changes the design or colors of any Solana Mark. This is the document's only hard visual rule: the "S" Logomark and word mark must be reproduced exactly as provided, no recoloring, no distortion, no redesign. Lesson: on a build, drop in the official logo file untouched; never rebuild it in CSS/SVG with tweaked proportions or your own palette.

SOLANA BRAND KIT, NO IMPLIED ENDORSEMENT: Any use that may be confusing, false, misleading, or that suggests sponsorship, endorsement, or affiliation with the Solana Foundation is prohibited unless you have explicit written permission from the Foundation. Likewise banned: combining any Solana Mark with any third-party design, trademark, graphic, text, or other element, including your own name, company logos, word marks, or product/service names, without explicit written permission. Lesson: never lock up a client logo next to the Solana mark or write "partnered with Solana" on a site without written sign-off.

SOLANA BRAND KIT, LANGUAGE AND NAMING RULES: The Marks may not be used descriptively of any products or services, and may not be used as a noun or verb; Solana Marks function as trademarks (adjectives) only. Uses that are defamatory, libelous, obscene, scandalous, or otherwise objectionable are prohibited, as is anything depicting hatred, intolerance, violence, or cruelty that could constitute hate speech or infringe others' rights. Lesson: in site copy write "the Solana network" or "built on Solana," never "a Solana" or "we Solana'd it."

SOLANA BRAND KIT, COMMERCIAL AND LEGAL PROHIBITIONS: Unauthorized commercial exploitation is banned, notably selling merchandise bearing any Solana Mark, without explicit written permission. Also prohibited: any use that damages or brings disrepute to the Foundation, network, or community; infringes, dilutes, depreciates, or impairs the Foundation's IP rights; or violates any law, regulation, public policy, or is connected to illegal activity. Lesson: if a client wants Solana-branded swag or paid products, that requires written Foundation permission before you design it.

SOLANA BRAND KIT, PERMISSIONS CONTACT: All exceptions run through one channel: questions about the Guidelines or requests for written permission for a specific project go to operations@solana.org. Enforcement is at the Foundation's sole discretion, and the Guidelines themselves may be updated from time to time, so compliance is a moving target tied to the current Brand and Press page. Lesson: when in doubt on a Solana-adjacent build, email operations@solana.org for written permission and re-check the live Brand and Press page for current assets.

# Solana.com Design System (RAG Knowledge Card)

SOLANA'S COLOR SYSTEM: The entire site sits on pure black (body background rgb(0,0,0), also #121212 and near-black tints #0B0A10 and #080d17 for section panels) with white #fff body text softened to rgb(208,208,220) for paragraphs. On top of that dark canvas sit exactly two signature brand accents, purple #9945FF and mint green #14F195, plus supporting neons cyan #80ecff, blue #64a8f2, and pink #f087ff/#eb54bb used only inside gradients. Muted grays #232323, #6c6a81, #ABABBA, and low-alpha whites like #ffffff14 and #ECE4FD1F handle borders and dividers. Lesson for builders: a dark-mode brand feels premium when 95 percent of the page is black and gray and the saturated accents are rationed to two hero hues that appear mostly as gradients, never as flat fills on large areas.

SOLANA'S GRADIENT LANGUAGE: Gradients are the brand's real logo. The canonical strokes are linear-gradient(225deg, #14f195 16.12%, #64a8f2 49.58%, #9945ff 83.36%) and linear-gradient(267.25deg, #9946ff 2.48%, #15f195 95.66%), a green-to-blue-to-purple sweep applied to headline text, button borders, and glowing card edges. Secondary gradient families run pink to violet, linear-gradient(100.51deg, #f087ff 27.61%, #6e1fce 62.14%, transparent), and cyan to blue, linear-gradient(100.22deg, #1fcff1 30.11%, #234cb6 66.43%, transparent), always fading to transparent at one end so they look like light beams rather than filled shapes. There are also vertical fade masks like linear-gradient(180deg, rgba(255,255,255,0)) and #121212-to-transparent overlays that melt imagery into the black background. Lesson: define two or three named multi-stop gradients with fixed angles and stop percentages and reuse them verbatim everywhere so the brand stays recognizable.

SOLANA'S TYPOGRAPHY STACK: Body copy is the custom sans Diatype at font-size 1.3125rem (21px) with line-height 140 percent and color #fff on black, with a fallback stack of Helvetica, Arial, system-ui. Big numeric stats (50M, 3.5B, $3.3T) switch to a custom brand monospace DSemi via a .font-brand-mono class (font-family: DSemi, monospace), and code uses ABC Mono or Roboto Mono; Space Grotesk and Monument exist as secondary display faces. Lesson: one workhorse grotesque for everything plus a distinctive mono reserved exclusively for numbers and data gives a fintech or crypto site instant technical credibility without font chaos.

SOLANA'S TYPE SCALE: The hero h1 uses font-size clamp(3.5rem, 8vw, 6rem) at weight 700, line-height 100 percent, letter-spacing -0.03rem, so it fluidly scales from 56px on mobile to 96px on desktop with zero breakpoint jumps. Section h2s use clamp(2.625rem, 8vw, 4.5rem) with letter-spacing -0.0425rem, and the tokenized nd-heading classes step 2xl from 40px to 56px to 88px (letter-spacing tightening from -1.2px to -2.64px), xl from 40px to 80px, and l from 32px to 64px, all at weight 500 with line-height 1.0 to 1.25. Lesson: pair every size increase with tighter negative letter-spacing and line-height near 1.0, and use clamp() with an 8vw preferred value so headlines feel massive but never overflow.

SOLANA'S SHAPE AND RADIUS SYSTEM: A base token --radius: 0.5rem drives calc(var(--radius) - 2px) and calc(var(--radius) - 4px) for nested elements, while cards and media step up through 0.75rem, 1rem, 16px, 18px, 20px, and 30px, and pills and buttons use 9999px full-round (buttons also carry --btn-border-radius: 6px). Hairline borders are 1px in low-alpha white like #ffffff14 or #ECE4FD33 rather than gray, so edges glow faintly against black. Lesson: derive inner radii from the outer container radius minus the padding delta (the minus 2px and minus 4px calc pattern) so nested rounded corners always look concentric instead of mismatched.

SOLANA'S MOTION SYSTEM: The dominant easing is cubic-bezier(.4, 0, .2, 1) (Material standard ease, used 17 times) with micro-interactions at .15s (25 uses), .2s, and .3s; slower reveals run .5s to .7s and one expressive curve cubic-bezier(.16, 1, .3, 1) handles springy entrances. The body itself has transition: background-color .3s ease, letting whole sections crossfade their background as you scroll, and ambient loops like marquees and glows run 5s to 8s. Lesson: standardize on one ease and three durations (150ms hover, 300ms state, 600ms reveal) and reserve long 8s infinite animations for decorative background motion only.

SOLANA'S IMAGERY AND ART DIRECTION: There is no photography of people in the core layout; imagery is abstract 3D-rendered light streaks and geometric decor (decor-bg.webp, pattern-parallelogram-r.svg) placed as absolutely positioned, oversized, bleeding layers with classes like absolute bottom-[-100px] right-[-1000px] mix-blend-overlay opacity-40 and blurred glow plates like opacity-30 blur-[40px]. Real photos appear only inside content cards (event covers, blog heroes) always as object-cover z-0 fills behind text, and roughly 20 partner logos (Visa, PayPal, BlackRock, Western Union, Franklin Templeton) render as white monochrome SVGs in uniform-height logo walls. Lesson: keep hero art abstract and brand-gradient-colored, push it off-canvas with negative positioning and mix-blend-overlay so it reads as atmosphere, and confine literal photography to card thumbnails.

SOLANA'S INFORMATION ARCHITECTURE: The homepage order is a single bold claim h1 (The capital market for every asset on earth), then community proof (Meet Solana IRL events grid), then performance claims (The fastest growing and leading financial platform, with live stats in DSemi mono like 3.5B and $3.3T), then real-time activity (What's happening right now), then institutional trust (The world's largest institutions and fintechs are building on Solana with the logo wall), then content (Keep up with the latest, How we built this stories), then a participation CTA section (Build alongside our global community) split into five h4 actions: Validate the network, Secure by staking, Build an app, Contribute with code, Tokenize an asset. Lesson: sequence a landing page as claim, proof, momentum, trust, content, then a segmented call to action that names each audience's specific next step.

SOLANA'S SIGNATURE MOVES: The three moves that make the site instantly recognizable are the green-to-purple 225deg gradient applied to text and glowing edges on a pure black field, giant clamp()-scaled Diatype headlines with aggressive negative tracking, and DSemi monospace stat counters presented as proof points. Everything decorative fades to transparent (gradients, image masks, section crossfades), so color always behaves like emitted light rather than paint. Lesson: to clone this energy, pick one dark canvas, one two-color gradient identity, one display face with tight tracking, and let every accent dissolve to transparency instead of ending in a hard edge.

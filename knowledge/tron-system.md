# TRON (tron.network) Design System

TRON'S COLOR SYSTEM: The entire site runs on a brutally small token set declared as CSS custom properties: --color-main-red: #EA0029, --color-light-red: #C60023, --color-font: #111111, --color-black: #000000, --color-dark-grey: #2e2e2e, --color-light-grey: #868686, --color-white: #FFFFFF. Page background is #F5F5F5 (light warm grey) with cards in #f8f8f8 and pure #fff section bands; text is #111 at full strength and rgba(0,0,0,.6) or rgba(17,17,17,.6) for secondary copy. Red #EA0029 is used almost exclusively for CTAs, hover accents, and one faint wash, never as a background field. Lesson for a builder: one saturated signal color on a near-monochrome grey/white/ink base reads as premium restraint; name the tokens and never freehand hex values outside them.

TRON'S TYPE SYSTEM: One family does everything: Poppins (weights 300, 400, 500, 600, 700 self-hosted as woff2 with font-display: swap, fallback PingFang SC then sans-serif), with Poppins/Inter/PingFang for numerals. Section headings are 48px weight 600, line-height 1.21, letter-spacing -0.02em, color #111 on desktop, dropping to 30px weight 600 with letter-spacing -0.0278em on mobile; a dark-band variant is 50px weight 600 in white. Feature card names are 24px weight 500; body and descriptions are 14px weight 400 line-height 1.5 in rgba(0,0,0,.6); buttons are 16px weight 600. Lesson: a two-step heading scale (48/30) plus 24px card titles and 14px body is enough for a whole marketing site, and negative letter-spacing around -0.02em is what makes a geometric sans like Poppins look engineered rather than bubbly.

TRON'S STAT NUMERALS: The proof-bar stats (Total Accounts, Total Transactions, Block Height, 24h Transfer Volume) are set at 44px in weight 300, the lightest weight on the site, with font-variant-numeric: tabular-nums and font-feature-settings "tnum", letter-spacing -0.03125em, over 14px grey labels, and they count up live (CountUp with duration 1s, easing disabled) inside a section washed with linear-gradient(180deg, rgba(234,0,41,0) 30%, rgba(234,0,49,.05)) over #f5f5f5. Lesson: make big numbers LIGHTER in weight than headings, force tabular figures so digits do not jitter while animating, and let the brand color appear only as a 5% opacity gradient wash behind the data.

TRON'S SIGNATURE PILL BUTTON: Every CTA is a fully round pill: border-radius 9999px, padding 14px 36px, 16px/600 Poppins, in four variants driven by custom properties, primary (--pill-bg: #EA0029, white text), white, ghost (transparent with 1px #dbdbdb border), and outline (white with 1px rgba(17,17,17,.15) border), and ALL variants share --pill-bg-hover: #111111, so every button turns ink-black on hover with transition background-color .15s ease. On hover a particle ink effect fires: radial-gradient(circle 90px at var(--pill-mx) var(--pill-my), ...) masks plus radial-gradient(circle, var(--pill-particle) .5px, transparent .8px) dot fields tracked to the cursor position. Lesson: pick one button silhouette, express variants only through CSS variables, and converge every hover to a single dark state; the cursor-tracked particle fill is TRON's most recognizable micro-interaction.

TRON'S MOTION LANGUAGE: The house easing is cubic-bezier(.16,1,.3,1), a strong ease-out-expo, used on the RiseInView word-by-word reveal where each word starts opacity 0 and translateY(.6em) and transitions opacity .8s and transform .8s on that curve; secondary moves use cubic-bezier(.22,.61,.36,1) at .55s and cubic-bezier(.7,0,.2,1) at .3s for panel slides. Utility transitions stay fast and plain: color and background shifts at .15s to .25s ease. Everything respects reduced motion by zeroing transitions. Lesson: split motion into two tiers, expressive entrances on a long expo-out curve (600 to 800ms) and functional state changes at 150 to 250ms ease, and animate headlines per word, not per block.

TRON'S CARD AND RADIUS SCALE: Structural sections are inset cards: the ecosystem stage uses a full-width card of #f8f8f8 clipped with clip-path: inset(0 20px round 16px), mobile section wraps use margin: 0 10px with border-radius 10px on #f8f8f8, small UI uses 4px, and pills use 9999px. Content sits in a 1200px fixed column (stats stretch to max-width 1320px with 60px side padding), and sections breathe with roughly 100px top and 112px bottom padding. Lesson: the radius vocabulary is exactly 4px (controls), 10 to 16px (section cards), full-round (buttons); floating grey cards inset from the viewport edge read far more modern than edge-to-edge background stripes.

TRON'S INFO ARCHITECTURE: Homepage order is: hero (an 800px-tall reserved slot for the animated "Decentralize the Web" banner with Build and Explore CTAs), live network stats bar, TRON Ecosystem (a sticky 480px card with an interactive globe canvas, a 48px title pinned at left 100px top 60px, and a rotating spotlight of products like JustLend DAO, SunPump, TronLink, USDD each with 24px name and 14px desc), EVM Compatible / Familiar SDK developer grid (TVM, DApp tools, TronScan, GasFree, Bug Bounty with Developer Hub and GitHub buttons), ViewPoint editorial list (48px title, dated items, feature image), Core Partners marquee (Samsung, Opera, Swisscom, Poloniex, MEXC logos in logoGrey and logoColor pairs that colorize on activation), then Insights news cards. Lesson: proof (live data) comes immediately after the promise (hero), ecosystem before tooling, and social proof (partners, press) last; grayscale partner logos that gain color on hover keep the logo wall from polluting the palette.

TRON'S ART DIRECTION: Imagery is not photography; it is an interactive 3D globe rendered to canvas inside the ecosystem card, flat single-color SVG product glyphs shipped in paired states (icon.svg grey default plus icon-color.svg or icon-red.svg active, e.g. gasfree.svg and gasfree-red.svg, samsung.svg and samsung-color.svg), and one webp editorial image for Insights. Descriptions under scrollable masks fade out via mask-image: linear-gradient(180deg, #000 70px, transparent), and marquees fade at their edges with linear-gradient(90deg, transparent 0, #000 6%, #000 94%, transparent). The mood is clinical fintech: white and grey fields, ink typography, red reserved for action, motion supplying the energy that color withholds. Lesson: on data-heavy brands, replace stock photos with one hero 3D or generative object plus a disciplined two-state icon system, and use gradient masks instead of hard clips wherever content overflows.

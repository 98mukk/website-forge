Temperament

Built for apartments and attention.

Frenchies want proximity more than acreage. They are content on 20-minute walks and thrive as long as you are within reach.

  • Low exercise needs, high companionship needs
  • Rarely barks, but will grumble opinions at you
  • Gets along with cats, kids and calmer dogs
Honest care

The flat face needs a plan.

We will not pretend this breed is low maintenance. You get a written guide covering heat, weight and airway health before you commit.

  • Keep them cool: no midday summer walks
  • Clean the nose rope and tail pocket weekly
  • Budget roughly $1,900 a year for good insurance and vet care

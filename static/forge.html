Five moves. Same order, every time.

The Forge is a fixed sequence, not a mood. Every engagement runs the same pipeline, so every delivery holds the same standard.

Forge the kit

Palette, type pairing, voice, and spacing rules are struck first and sealed into a brand kit. Everything downstream obeys it.

output: brand-kit.html + design tokens

Paint the imagery

Original imagery is painted for your site by an AI art department, directed by the kit. No stock photos, no gray placeholder boxes.

output: hero + section imagery, yours outright

Build the pages

Four pages are written against the kit on one shared CSS system, consulting a design memory built from sites we consider proven.

output: home, about, services, contact

Face the judge

A ruthless design judge scores the build from 1 to 10 against our no slop laws and names every flaw. Under the bar, it goes back to the fire and gets rebuilt.

output: a written scorecard, included in delivery

Take delivery

Pages, kit, and imagery are packaged into a single zip. Host it anywhere. No lock in, no subscription, no dependency on us.

output: forge-package.zip

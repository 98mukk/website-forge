Co-branding

Partner logos sit beside our mark, separated by a thin vertical divider with 2X gap on each side. Horizontal lockup preferred, vertical as fallback. The whole lockup follows the same rule: white on dark, black on light.

Logo don'ts
Do not recolor or gradient the mark. Ship white-on-dark or black-on-light only.
Do not redraw or stretch the wordmark. Use live type or the official SVG.
Do not place on low-contrast surfaces or busy photos.
Do not add drop shadows, bevels, or glass effects.
Do not rotate the lockup or break the icon-to-wordmark gap.
Do not fill the "O" dot with any color but accent teal.

60 / 30 / 9 / 1. Base carries the canvas, surface lifts cards, accent marks the single action, signal is reserved for one alert or status per view.

Light & dark surfaces

The system is dark-first. On rare light surfaces use ink #0B0F14 for text and keep accent teal at button scale only, never as page fill.

Accessibility notes

Ink #EAF0F5 on base clears 15.8:1. Accent #2FD4A3 on base clears 8.9:1 for large text and UI. Never set accent on signal, and never use signal for body copy.

Mood & subjects

Low-key night interiors of real small businesses lit by a single teal screen glow. Owners at closing time, hands on a counter, a dashboard reflecting in a window. Quiet, confident, lived-in.

Treatment

Deep shadows, teal-cool grade, single key light. Grain kept subtle. Crop tight on hands and screens. Subject placed off-center, never dead middle.

Usage Rules

How to keep it premium.

06 / 06

Do

  • Keep accent to roughly 9 percent of any layout, on actions and status.
  • Load Sora and IBM Plex Sans as live type from Google Fonts.
  • Give sections generous whitespace and one clear focal point.
  • Pull logo files from the official kit, white on dark by default.
  • Use organic numbers and full name plus role on every quote.
  • Give interactive elements a visible accent focus ring.

Don't

  • Do not use accent as a background wallpaper or full fill.
  • Do not add a second loud accent to compete with teal.
  • Do not ship purple gradients, glass blur, or drop shadows.
  • Do not center the hero subject or use em-dashes in copy.
  • Do not put thin colored bars on dark cards.
  • Do not render the wordmark as a flat image instead of live type.
